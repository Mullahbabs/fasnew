Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase items from designers on Fashub?

Browse designer collections, select your desired items, and click "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout where you'll enter shipping details. Note: Payment processing requires our upcoming app version (coming soon!).

Can I really connect with local tailors through Fashub?

Absolutely! Our Tailor Connect feature shows verified local tailors in your area. View their portfolios, request quotes, and schedule fittings directly through the platform.

How does the virtual fashion show work?

Designers can upload 360¬∞ views of their collections. Users "attend" scheduled shows in real-time or watch recordings, with options to click on showcased items for immediate purchase.

Is there a fee to sell my designs as a reseller?

Fashub takes a minimal 5% commission only after a successful sale. No upfront costs - you simply create a profile, upload items with photos/descriptions, and set your prices.

How do I customize and order my own designs?

Use our Design Studio tool to mix fabrics, patterns, and silhouettes. When satisfied, submit your design. We'll match you with a tailor or manufacturer and provide a quote within 48 hours.
